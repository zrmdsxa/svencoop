G-Man Towers
=========

You are a member of a secret organization of Black Mesa Civilians who, during the Black Mesa catastrophe, teamed up against the incoming aliens (and military) in a bid to escape the facility, and, to an extent, have succeeded. 

It is now two weeks after your escape and you and your team have been called upon by the Administrator in order to discuss the prospect of a strategic alliance. You are now about to enter the Administrators office, where he is to discuss your fate...
